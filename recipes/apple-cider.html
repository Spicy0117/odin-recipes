Apple Cider

A Drink for the Whimsical or the bold if you have the constitution

Want to warm youself and get a bit tipsy after a long day of adventuring?

A hot and spicy beverage that could warm the soul of the coldest ice giant

Ingredients

  • 2 Quarts Apple Cider
  • 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1Lemon Grated
  • 1Orange Grated
  • 8Cloves
  • 5Cinnomon Sticks
  • 12Allspice Berries

    • Light Rum optional Its better if you add it and just pour till you think its good

Instructions

  1. If you have a cheesecloth at hand, wrap lemon peel, orange peel, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and allspice berries into a pouch. If you do not have a cheesecloth, you can add these ingredients to cider, but you will have to strain them out later.
  2. In a stainless steel pan, combine cider, brown sugar, and spices. Bring to a boil and turn to a very light simmer and simmer on low for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and allow to cool until safe enough to drink. Add alcohol and serve with apple slices.
